CONTAINCORP

A Storydriven Management Simulation Game inspired by the SCP Universe. Containcorp has you managing a top-secret worldwide government-run facility that specialises in containing and experimenting on anomalous entities. Set in its own universe but inspired by the SCP universe, you will encounter a multitude of anomalous objects and entities. What you learn about each and how is up to you...

ABOUT US

Plasmarc Studios is a dynamic duo of passionate programmers committed to crafting captivating games. As a recently established studio, we bring a fresh perspective and an abundance of enthusiasm to deliver exceptional products. Despite being a small team, we strive to create immersive experiences that defy expectations and showcase our dedication to quality. Founded on August 26th, 2020, initially as a complement to our main game Containcorp, we eagerly anticipate the exciting journey that lies ahead.

MEET THE TEAM

Hey there! This is David! I came up with the concept and got the ball rolling, so... I'm pretty big news around here. I studied Computer Science at Exeter University, graduating with a First which is pretty neat all things considered. Since it's my dream to become a successful indie game developer, I figured it was about time I released a game. So I started this project!

I started making games since I was a wee-little 14 year old. They were all crap, understandably. But nowadays, I seem to be able to make slightly less crap games ;P. In all seriousness, it's pretty surreal seeing myself today. If I told myself back then that I would be making a game of this scale, I would have probably had a heart attack, because time travel is not a normal daily occurrence and my weak 14 year old heart probably couldn't take that.

I've been working on this game for a few years now, and I'm really excited to finally release it!

Hi y'all, I'm Dillan! I studied Comp Sci at the Uni of Manchester, graduating with a first class degree. I started Containcorp with David during the early days of COVID-19 lockdown. What initially was a 10 week plan to release a game with 0 experience turned into quite the project huh!

Since graduating, I've been working as a software engineer for Tesco, and trying to balance working on the game and handling the admin side of running a small studio. At this point, I can't wait for Containcorp to get out there - it's been 5 long and slightly laboured years, but we've hit our first of many goals - a pre-alpha release!

So far, I've been adding horrendous code within our codebase, as well as developing our online presence, through our game's website, wiki page and merch store!
